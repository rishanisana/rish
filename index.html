How Love & Aesthetics works




As interior designers, we know how time consuming it is to find furniture and decor that’s actually worth writing home about. But thanks to our experience sourcing pieces for hundreds of thousands of design projects annually, we've sharpened our collective ability to spot a best-in-class find. After 10 years of trend spotting, internet sleuthing, and product testing, we know exactly which products will make a space sing.

Why choose L&A


  • BEST DESIGNERS

    Match with one of our talented designers and get inspired. With 10 years of experience in all 50 states and 100,000+ designs, we’ll help you hone your style, get inspired, and realize your design dreams for your specific space.

  • COLLABORATION

    Collaborate 1:1 with your designer to create your perfect space. Let your designer guide you to a design that meets your functional needs and suits your unique style. See it come to life with a 3D rendering of the design in your actual space.

  • DREAM HOME

    Bring your design home with ease. Our designers source from 100+ home brands so no two spaces are ever alike. Leverage Havenly’s proprietary platform with a simple consolidated checkout and never overpay with our price guarantee.
